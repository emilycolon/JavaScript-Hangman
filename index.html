The first player will pick a word that the second player will try to guess letter by letter. Every wrong letter guessed will result in a "piece" of the hanged man added to the gallows. Try to guess the word before he swings!

When picking a word you can only choose a single word that does not include punctuation. You will win the game if the guesser is unable to guess the word before the man is hanged.

When guessing, your goal is to try to guess the word one letter at a time. Wrong guesses will result in more of the hanged man added to the gallows. Correct guesses will reveal more of the submitted word. Don't worry, you won't be penalized if you choose the same letter more than once! You will win the game if you are able to guess the word before the man is hanged.

Hangman

Get ready to play some Hangman!

  • Instructions

Player One will pick a word that Player Two will try to guess letter by letter. Every wrong letter guessed will result in a "piece" of the hanged man to be added to the gallows. Try to guess the word before he swings!

Player One: You can only choose a single word that does not include punctuation. You will win the game if Player Two is unable to guess the word before the man is hanged.

Player Two: Try to guess the word one letter at a time. Wrong guesses will result in more of the hanged man to be added to the gallows. Correct guesses will reveal more of the submitted word. Don't worry, you won't be penalized if you choose the same letter more than once! You will win the game if you are able to guess the word before the man is hanged.

Player One: Enter a word in the text box below and click the Submit button.

Player Two: Enter a letter into the text box below and click the submit button in order to try to guess the word.
